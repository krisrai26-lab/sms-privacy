# SMS Privacy Policy — Kristopher Rai (personal assistant line "Mila")

_Last updated: July 23, 2026_

This policy covers the low-volume SMS program operated by Kristopher Rai
through his personal assistant line, Mila, for scheduling, reminders, and
personal or professional logistics. It does not send marketing or
promotional messages.

**How consent is obtained.** People opt in through the public
[Mila SMS opt-in page](https://krisrai26-lab.github.io/sms-privacy/opt-in.html).
After reviewing the SMS disclosure and actively selecting an unchecked
consent box, the person sends **START** from their mobile number to
**+1 (786) 833-8144**. The inbound START message records consent. Numbers are
not enrolled through verbal consent, imported contact lists, purchased,
rented, or scraped lists.

**Information collected.** The program stores the mobile number, the inbound
START consent message and timestamp, and message content needed to continue
the requested conversation.

**How information is used.** Information is used only for one-to-one
scheduling, reminders, and personal or professional logistics involving
Kristopher Rai.

**Sharing.** No mobile information or SMS consent data is sold, rented, or
shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional
purposes.

**Frequency and rates.** Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may
apply.

**Opting out and help.** Reply **STOP** at any time to opt out. Reply
**HELP** for assistance or email krisrai26@gmail.com.

**Contact.** krisrai26@gmail.com
